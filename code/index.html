Fact: Over 2 billion people globally struggle with water insecurity.

What You Can Do

Spreading awareness and cultivating donations from campus is the first step in this project. Generating these resources starts with you.

Where We Come In

Your work here is done. We pool your resources into valuable social programs and charity services that aim to better the lives of people all around the world. Resources go towards maintaining infrastructure, healthcare, and basic human health in disadvantaged areas.

Tangible Change

This is where your work manifests! We have boots on the ground in water-scarce areas that implement all of the changes and support that your contributions make possible! Thanks to you, billions have a fighting chance to live healthily.

Safe drinking water is much more than a basic human need. It's the foundation for improved health, education, and economic opportunity. Where clean water flows, communities flourish. At Charity:Water, it's our mission to bring clean drinking water to every person on the planet.
